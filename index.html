Lecture Notes

  • Introduction to spatial heterogeneity and non-stationarity
  • Difference between stationary and non-stationary relationships
  • Why spatial relationships vary across place
  • Global models versus local models
  • Geographically Weighted Regression (GWR) in forensic GIS
  • How to evaluate GWR compared with OLS
  • Case study on overdose mortality and socioeconomic factors
  • Interpretation of Moran’s I and GWR results
  • Why GWR supports place-specific forensic reasoning
